Computes the weak nuclear charge \( Q_W = N - (1 - 4\sin^2\theta_W)Z \) for the target nucleus In the expression above, \( N \) ( \(Z\)) is the neutron (proton) number of the target nucleus and \( \theta_W \) is the weak mixing angle.
